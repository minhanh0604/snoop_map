Our Mission

Discover how your dog smells by exploring the scents of food.

Some foods may smell marvelous to both of you but are harmful to your dog. We let you know which foods are safe to feed your pups and which are not.

Explore the smells around your local dog parks.

A holistic lifestyle includes eating a wholesome diet, and OF COURSE, regular exercise for your dog. We provide you with a map of scents around your local dog parks. Snooping around the world is just as fun for you as it is for your pups.

*Currently, we only provide a map of dog parks in New York. Be on the lookout as we expand to other cities.
